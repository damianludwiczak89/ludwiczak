Welcome,


My name is Damian Ludwiczak, I am from Konin, Poland. I am a self-taught software engineer. In 2012, I graduated from Państwowa Wyższa Szkoła Zawodowa w Konine in the field of English Filology with Informational Technology. My strong sides are Python, Django, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and SQL.

For the last couple of years I have been working in a transport company, where I have been promoted to office manager. I manage a fleet of over 20 trucks, supervise transports, truck service schedule etc. I also stay in touch with customers, negotiate with them in foreign language, inform about deliveries. Besides that, I distribute tasks to the office team.

When in 2022 I found my passion for coding and computer science, I decided to change careers, and become a self-taught software engineer. I did my own research and set my learning path. I participated in a series of Harvard's courses called CS50. My whole learning has been in English. I see programming as an never-ending journey to keep getting better and improving my skills.

Being a self-taught defines some characteristics as a person. I am highly motivated, self-disciplined and willing to learn. Additionally, my experience as an office manager in a transport company helped me develops soft skills like communication, teamwork, time-management etc. Personally, I am an open and positive person.
